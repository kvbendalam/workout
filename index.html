Essential Training Tips
📈
Progressive Overload: Add 2.5kg to compound lifts every 1–2 weeks or add 1 rep before adding weight. Keep a logbook — if you're not tracking, you're not progressing.
💧
Hydration: Drink 500ml water before training, sip during, and 750ml+ post-workout. Dehydration reduces strength by up to 15%.
🥗
Nutrition: Eat 1.8–2.2g protein per kg bodyweight daily. Have 30–40g carbs + 25–30g protein within 60 min post-workout. Chicken, eggs, paneer, dal are your friends.
😴
Sleep: 7–9 hours is when 80% of muscle repair happens. GH (growth hormone) spikes during deep sleep. Poor sleep kills gains faster than poor training.
🧠
Mind-Muscle Connection: For isolation exercises, slow the eccentric (lowering) to 3–4 seconds. Feel the muscle stretch, not just move the weight.
🔁
Deload every 4–6 weeks: Drop volume by 40% for one week. You'll come back stronger — overtraining is a real plateau killer.
