🏋️ WORKOUT PLAN

💪 Monday — Full Body A

Total-Body Strength

🔥 Warm-Up (10 min)

🚶 Light Cardio5 min
Easy pace to raise heart rate and warm joints.
🤸 Dynamic Stretching2 rounds
Arm circles, leg swings, hip openers. Dynamic not static.
🎯 Band Pull-Apart2×15
Shoulder/scapular prep — do before any pressing.

💪 Strength Training

🏋️ Barbell Back Squat ⚡3×10–12
Type: Compound · Rest: 60s · Muscles: Quads, Glutes
Form:
  1. Bar on upper traps. Feet shoulder-width, toes slightly out.
  2. Big breath. 360° brace.
  3. Break at hips AND knees together.
  4. Descend until hip crease BELOW knee.
  5. Drive through heels, chest tall, push knees out.
⚠️ Avoid:
  • Knees caving in — ACL risk
  • Heels rising — ankle mobility issue
  • Half squats — wastes the exercise
  • Hips shoot up faster than chest
💡 Tips:
  • Pause squat (3 sec bottom) eliminates bounce
  • Try high bar for quads, low bar for glutes
🦵 Leg Press3×10–12
Type: Compound · Rest: 60s · Muscles: Quads, Glutes
Form:
  1. Feet shoulder-width on plate. Back flat against pad.
  2. Unrack, lower sled to 90° knee angle in 3 sec.
  3. Drive through full foot back up.
  4. Don't lock knees at top.
⚠️ Avoid:
  • Knees caving in
  • Locking knees — can hyperextend
💡 Tips:
  • High feet = more glute, low feet = more quad
🏋️ Flat Barbell Bench Press3×10–12
Type: Compound · Rest: 60s · Muscles: Chest, Triceps
Form:
  1. Lie flat, eyes under bar. Grip 1.5× shoulder width.
  2. Retract shoulder blades into bench, plant feet firmly.
  3. Unrack bar to over upper chest. Big breath, brace core.
  4. Lower bar to nipple line in 3 seconds — elbows at 45–75°.
  5. Pause 1 sec at chest, no bounce. Press up explosively.
⚠️ Avoid:
  • Elbows flared 90° — shoulder impingement risk
  • Bouncing bar off chest — injury risk
  • Hips lifting off bench — reduces ROM
💡 Tips:
  • Drive feet into floor for leg drive
  • Try "bend the bar" cue to activate lats
💪 Incline Dumbbell Press3×10–12
Type: Compound · Rest: 60s · Muscles: Upper Chest, Front Delts
Form:
  1. Set bench to 30°–45°. Kick dumbbells up with knees.
  2. Lower DBs to outer upper chest — full stretch.
  3. Hold 1 sec, press up and slightly in.
  4. Squeeze at top. Don't clang DBs together.
⚠️ Avoid:
  • Bench too steep (>45°) — becomes front delt exercise
  • Incomplete stretch at bottom — misses upper chest
💡 Tips:
  • 30° = more chest, 45° = more delt
  • Supinate wrists slightly at top for peak squeeze
🚶 Walking Lunges2×12–15
Type: Functional · Rest: 30s · Muscles: Quads, Glutes
Form:
  1. Stand tall with DBs at sides.
  2. Long step forward. Lower rear knee to 1" above ground.
  3. Front knee tracks over middle of foot.
  4. Drive through front heel into next step.
  5. Keep torso vertical throughout.
⚠️ Avoid:
  • Front knee past toes
  • Short stride — becomes a squat
  • Looking down
💡 Tips:
  • Long stride = more glute. Short = more quad

🔥 Wednesday — Full Body B

Posterior Chain Focus

🔥 Warm-Up (10 min)

🚶 Light Cardio5 min

🤸 Dynamic Stretching2 rounds

🎯 Band Pull-Apart2×15

💪 Strength Training

🏋️ Romanian Deadlift ⚡3×10–12
Type: Compound · Rest: 60s · Muscles: Hamstrings, Glutes
Form:
  1. Stand with bar at hip height, overhand grip.
  2. Push hips BACK — like closing a drawer.
  3. Bar drags down legs — shins, then knees.
  4. Lower until hamstring stretch stops you.
  5. Drive hips forward to stand, squeeze glutes.
⚠️ Avoid:
  • Bending knees like a squat — this is a hip hinge
  • Rounding lower back — never under load
  • Bar drifting forward
💡 Tips:
  • Point tailbone to ceiling to maintain neutral spine
💀 Conventional Deadlift3×10–12
Type: Compound · Rest: 60s · Muscles: Hamstrings, Glutes, Lower Back
Form:
  1. Bar over mid-foot. Hip-width stance.
  2. Hip hinge, grip bar just outside legs.
  3. Big breath. Shoulders slightly in front of bar.
  4. PUSH THE FLOOR AWAY — don't "pull up".
  5. Lock knees and hips together at top.
  6. Lower slowly, reset each rep.
⚠️ Avoid:
  • Rounding lower back — most dangerous
  • Bar drifting from legs
  • Jerking off floor
  • Looking up excessively
💡 Tips:
  • "Push the floor" cue instantly fixes 90% of issues
  • Mixed grip once double-overhand fails
⬆️ Weighted Pull-up3×10–12
Type: Compound · Rest: 60s · Muscles: Lats, Biceps
Form:
  1. Hang with shoulder-width grip, arms fully extended.
  2. Retract shoulder blades first, then pull.
  3. Drive elbows to hip pockets. Chin over bar.
  4. Lower in 3 seconds to dead hang.
⚠️ Avoid:
  • Half reps — no dead hang stretch
  • Kipping — momentum not muscle
  • Too wide grip — reduces ROM
💡 Tips:
  • Imagine crushing oranges in your armpits
  • Add weighted belt once BW is easy
⬇️ Lat Pulldown3×10–12
Type: Compound · Rest: 60s · Muscles: Lats
Form:
  1. Grip bar wider than shoulders. Secure thighs under pad.
  2. Lean back slightly (15°). Drive elbows down.
  3. Pull bar to upper chest. Squeeze lats for 1 sec.
  4. Let bar rise with control, arms fully extending.
⚠️ Avoid:
  • Pulling behind the neck — cervical spine risk
  • Using biceps more than lats — pull with elbows
  • No lat stretch at top — limits growth
💡 Tips:
  • Think: crack a walnut between your shoulder blades
🦵 Seated Leg Curl2×12–15
Type: Isolation · Rest: 30s · Muscles: Hamstrings
Form:
  1. Adjust seat so knee aligns with machine pivot.
  2. Grip handles. Curl legs down fully.
  3. Hold 1 sec at contraction.
  4. Extend in 3 seconds — slow eccentric.
⚠️ Avoid:
  • Wrong seat adjustment — knee stress
  • Letting stack crash — loses eccentric
  • Partial ROM
💡 Tips:
  • Seated version stretches hamstring more than lying
