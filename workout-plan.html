Key Tips for Wedding Prep
💧
Drink 3–4 litres of water daily, especially on leg days. Hydration directly impacts fat metabolism.
🥗
Target ~150–160g protein/day (chicken, eggs, paneer, dal). Aim for a 400–500 cal daily deficit.
😴
7–8 hours of sleep is non-negotiable. Most fat loss and muscle repair happens during deep sleep.
📈
Track weights every session. Progressive overload (even small increases) is what drives results week-over-week.
🧂
The week before May 10: cut sodium and processed foods. You'll look visibly leaner and less bloated on the day.
